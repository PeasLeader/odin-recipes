Simple-Custard

Description

This custard recipe is a combination of a few different recipes I've tried. It's not too rich or too sweet as many egg custards can be. Can be served warm immediately or cold.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1 table spoon vanila extract
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoon cornstarch

STEPS

  1. step 1

    2. gather all the ingredients

  2. Step 2

    3. Place milk, vanilla extract, and butter in a saucepan.

  3. Step 3

    4. Cook and stir over medium heat until simmering; remove from heat before it comes to a boil.

  4. Step 4

    5. Whisk eggs, sugar, and cornstarch together in a bowl until sugar dissolves.

  5. Step 5

    6. Set the saucepan back over low heat. Pour in egg mixture slowly,

    whisking constantly, until custard thickens enough to coat the bottom of a spoon, 5 to 10 minutes.

  6. Step 6

    7. Serve warm or transfer custard into a large bowl and cover.

  7. Step 7

    8. Allow to cool before chilling. Enjoy!
